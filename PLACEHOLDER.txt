## Image Requirements

- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Recommended size: 600x800 pixels (portrait orientation)
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 or similar
- File naming: Use lowercase with hyphens (e.g., my-product-name.jpg)

## How to Add New Product Images

1. Copy your image to this folder
2. Update the product entry in ../script.js with the correct filename

## How to Remove Product Images

1. Delete the image file from this folder
2. Remove the product entry from ../script.js
